Application Design Proposal

Kevin Shin-Wheeler

Graphic Communication 429-01

Winter 2022

Summary

For this project, I have created a personal life manager for individuals wishing to simplify how they keep track of their information and tasks. The goal of this application was to provide a level of organization where users can gather information in the least about the steps possible. Simultaneously, it aims to streamline the process of feeding important information into the interface to encourage users to enter anything that could need to be stored. In the following pages, you will be able to explore the process I went through leading up to a final design. I hope you enjoy.
