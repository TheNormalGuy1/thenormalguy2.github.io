About Me

I'm an Oregon-born visual artist studying comics at the School of Visual Arts in New York City. I work in a variety of genres and media, primarily ink and Photoshop. I'm always looking for opportunties to work in comics and to collaborate with other artists. My work has been featured in Epic Wayne Quarterly and Ink Magazine.
